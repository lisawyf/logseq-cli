Investigate lessons learned for "$ARGUMENTS" using my local Logseq graph.

Run:
- `logseq-cli cards build lesson "$ARGUMENTS" --json`
- `logseq-cli lessons list "$ARGUMENTS" --json`

Then produce:
1. The most reusable lessons or best practices.
2. The pitfalls or recurring mistakes to avoid.
3. The habits, checklists, or safeguards that seem worth standardizing.
4. A short "next time remember this" section.

Clearly distinguish retrieved facts from your synthesis.
